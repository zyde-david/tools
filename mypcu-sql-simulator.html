### 🎨 Summary of What Was Updated in the Live Canvas:

1. **AI Copilot Prominently on Top-Left:** Positioned on the top-left area as the primary user interface.
2. **Dedicated Map Modifier Button (`+ แผนที่ (พิกัด GIS)`):** Placed right inside the AI Copilot section so users can click to request GPS coordinates whenever map plotting is needed.
3. **No Map by Default (PDPA Safe):** Unless `"แผนที่"` is typed or the map modifier button is clicked, AI queries ignore `latitude`/`longitude` and focus on health attributes (like treatment rights `m_CARD`, age, village).
4. **Plain-Thai Explanation Breakdown:** Every search result provides a clear, plain-Thai explanation of **WHAT tables were fetched** (e.g., `m_CARD` for rights, `m_CHRONIC` for diabetes) and **WHY**.
5. **Full CRUD & Management for AI Modifiers:** Officers can click **"จัดการปุ่ม (CRUD)"** to add, edit, or delete custom search modifiers (e.g., adding `+ สิทธิ UC` or `+ ค่าบริการ`).
